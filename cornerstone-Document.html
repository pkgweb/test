What is Cornerstone?

Cornerstone is a distribution of Drupal with integrated performance, SEO, simpleness, and testing enhancements.

Cornerstone addresses a long-standing problem: High-traffic sites use stable versions of Drupal, and stable versions of Drupal are ineligible for enhancements to solve performance bottlenecks discovered after widespread deployment.
